WHAT HAPPENS INSIDE CNN:

1. Early convolution layers detect:
   - edges
   - textures
   - orientations

2. Middle layers combine patterns into:
   - shapes
   - contours
   - object parts

3. Final convolution layers detect:
   - class-specific structures
   - semantic object regions

4. Grad-CAM backtracks importance from:
   prediction → gradients → feature maps
5. Important kernels are the filters that
   generated influential activations.
